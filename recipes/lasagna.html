Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna

Descriptions

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Meat Sauce
  5. Parmesan cheese
  6. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degreees F. Cover with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
